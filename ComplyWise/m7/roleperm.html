Admin

  • Manage user accounts (add, edit, disable, or delete users).
  • Assign and modify roles (grant permissions to Users, Reviewers, or Admins).
  • View and edit system settings, including compliance rules and audit parameters.
  • Access detailed compliance reports for all users.
  • Resolve flagged documents and override Reviewer decisions if necessary.
  • Monitor system logs and security alerts.

Compliance Officer/Reviewer

  • Review flagged documents for compliance violations.
  • Provide feedback or request corrections from users on flagged documents.
  • Approve or reject resubmitted documents after review.
  • Access and manage compliance-related notifications and alerts.
  • Generate compliance reports for reviewed documents.
  • No permissions to edit roles, users, or system settings.

Employee

  • Submit documents for compliance checks.
  • View flagged documents and address issues by resubmitting corrected versions.
  • Access notifications related to their document submissions and account activities.
  • Manage personal account settings (e.g., update profile, change password, set notification preferences).
  • No permissions to access or manage other users, roles, or system settings.
